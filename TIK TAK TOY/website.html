How to Play

1

Choose Mode

Select from offline, computer, or online multiplayer modes based on your preference.

2

Start Game

Click on any empty cell to make your move. X goes first, followed by O.

3

Get Three in a Row

Be the first to get three of your marks in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally).

4

Win or Draw

Win by getting three in a row, or the game ends in a draw if all cells are filled.
